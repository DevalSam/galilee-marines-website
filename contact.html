Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can you respond to project inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent projects, we can provide initial consultation within 4-6 hours during business hours.

What regions do you serve?

We operate across 16 countries with primary focus in Nigeria, Latin America, and Europe. Our 20+ offices ensure local expertise wherever you need us.

Do you provide emergency marine services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency support for marine operations, vessel assistance, and urgent logistics requirements through our emergency hotline.

How can I get a project quote?

Submit your project details through our contact form, call our office, or email us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your needs and provide a detailed quote.

What certifications do you hold?

We are certified to ISO 9001, 14001, and OHSAS 18001 standards, ensuring quality, environmental responsibility, and safety in all operations.

Can you handle international projects?

Absolutely. With operations across three continents and experience in 16 countries, we're well-equipped to handle complex international marine and construction projects.
