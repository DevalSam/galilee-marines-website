Independent Oil & Gas Company

Operating as an independent oil and gas company, we have very deep roots in trading (import, export, distribution, sales and purchase, brokerage, marketing etc.) of petroleum products and commodities.

We are able to trade oil and gas globally due to technical alliances and strategic affiliations with refineries. We strive to meet local and international demands of energy products across the known world. In the various countries where we do business of supply of petroleum products, we use strategic storage facilities for effective storage and timely distribution.

Filling Station Operations

Managing a network of retail locations with excellence in customer service

Retail Network Management

In Nigeria, we play in the downstream market through the management of third party outlets while pursuing acquisition of self owned stations. Through this downstream operations we are able to deliver quality fuel products and services across Nigeria.

Retail Location Management

We operate managing a network of more than 20 retail locations primarily under different brands, as well as an extensive 24-hour diesel-stop network.

24/7 Service Network

Extensive 24-hour diesel-stop network ensuring continuous fuel availability for commercial and industrial customers.

Growth Strategy

The ongoing growth in our network of services provisioning and the pursuit of self-owned stations is a function of our niche approach of management.
