Who We Are

Galilee Marines Ltd is a Nigerian Construction, Environment and Marine Company and Consultant. We are proud of the reputation we have developed for creating new and innovative solutions to Construction, Environmental remediation, shipping, dredging and marine construction challenges.

With a presence in Nigeria stretching back well over two decades, we have built a strong portfolio of loyal customers locally and internationally. Our company has evolved from its former identity as Yessab Resources to become one of Nigeria's leading marine construction and logistics companies.

At Galilee Marines Ltd, we offer an extensive range of services spanning Construction, Consultancy, learning and development, dredging, shipping, cargo, logistics, marine construction and freight-related services across sixteen countries in Nigeria, Latin America and Europe.

Our Journey

Two decades of growth, innovation, and excellence

2003 - Foundation

Established as Yessab Resources, beginning our journey in marine and logistics services with a focus on the Nigerian market.

2008 - Expansion

Expanded operations to include construction services and established multiple offices across Nigerian states.

2012 - Certification

Achieved ISO 9001, 14001, and OHSAS 18001 certifications, establishing our commitment to quality and safety standards.

2015 - International Growth

Extended services to Latin America and Europe, becoming a truly international marine services provider.

2020 - Rebranding

Transformed into Galilee Marines Ltd, reflecting our evolved capabilities and expanded service portfolio.

2024 - Industry Leadership

Recognized as Nigeria's leading marine construction and logistics company, handling over 500 port calls annually.
